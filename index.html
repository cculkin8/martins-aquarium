My favorite locations

Key West, Florida

Key West is where my I start my fishing season. Though it may be open from May all the way to December to fish, in my experience you'll have the best luck in the beginning of May. Henry, my magnificent Grouper, was caught May of 2018 there.

Panama

Panama is a beautiful location to harvest in the summer months, I have personally had great luck finding beautiful Wahoo Tuna there. My lovely Chunky was caught June of 2020 there!

Belize

Belize is one of the best places to fish in the world; there is a great and hungry population of interesting fish year round to harvest. You'll find the best specimen midsummer to late fall, Trent the Tarpon was found there July of 2019.

Prince Edward Island, Canada

Prince Edward Island, (Canada's smallest province by both size and population,) is a great place to harvest respectable sized bluefin tuna. It's my recommendation top go fishing from August to November to get the best harvests.

Montauk, New York

Montauk, (often called The End of The World by its residents,) is a great place to find Bluefish from September to October; I caught my world famous Eagle there!
